Acutis Firewall is a parental control app that protects your family by blocking adult content, malware, and dangerous websites. All filtering happens locally on your device for maximum privacy.

KEY FEATURES

Content Protection
- Block adult and pornographic websites with a comprehensive blocklist of 128,000+ sites
- Protect against malware and phishing attacks with real-time DNS filtering
- Optional blocking for gambling sites and social media platforms
- Works across ALL apps, not just browsers

Time-Based Controls
- Set daily screen time limits for specific sites or categories
- Schedule blocking during homework time, bedtime, or family hours
- Choose which days of the week rules apply
- Flexible rules for domains, categories, or custom lists

Parental Controls
- PIN-protect settings so children cannot disable protection
- One-tap activation with no configuration required
- Auto-start protection when device boots
- Easy-to-use interface for managing blocklists

Privacy First
- All filtering happens on your device - no data sent to external servers
- No analytics, tracking, or data collection
- DNS queries go directly to Cloudflare (1.1.1.1) for fast, secure resolution
- PIN stored with Android's encrypted storage

HOW IT WORKS

Acutis Firewall creates a local VPN tunnel on your device to intercept DNS queries. When a blocked domain is requested, it returns "not found" - preventing the content from loading. Allowed traffic passes through normally with no slowdown.

BLOCKLIST CATEGORIES

- Adult Content: 128,000+ pornographic and explicit sites
- Malware & Phishing: Known dangerous domains updated regularly
- Gambling: Betting and gambling websites
- Social Media: Major social platforms (optional)
- Custom Lists: Add your own domains to block

PERFECT FOR

- Parents wanting to protect children from inappropriate content
- Families looking to reduce screen time and social media usage
- Anyone wanting protection from malware and phishing
- Schools and organizations managing device usage

REQUIREMENTS

- Android 10 or higher
- No root required
- No subscription - completely free
